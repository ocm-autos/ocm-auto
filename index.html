Service Codes

Four things. Done properly.

Every job we take runs through the same four stages — the same order, every time, so you always know where you stand.

OCM-101
Consultation
Describe the noise, the smell, the warning light — we talk it through before anything is booked in, so you're not paying to find out if it's worth paying.
OCM-102
Diagnosis
A full multi-point inspection and scan, translated out of mechanic-speak into a plain report you can actually read and act on.
OCM-103
Recommendation
What needs doing now, what can wait, and what's not worth the money at all — an honest ranking, not a upsell list.
OCM-104
Fixing
Once you've agreed the plan, our technicians carry out the repair — no surprise line items, no work you didn't sign off on.
